Study of Logic gates

The 5 volt DC power for the IC (74LS08) can be taken from the +5V socket or from OD1 (need to enable OD1). The inputs are connected to the square waves SQ1 and SQ2. The wave generator WG should be set to SQ2 option. The output is connected to A3 (via a series resistor because the range of A3 is 3.3 volts only). The voltage ranges of A1 and A2 are set to 16 volts and they are shifted for a clear view, by avoiding overlapping of traces.
Wiring Diagram   Photograph of the experimental setup

The yellow and green traces are inputs and red is the output. It can be seen that the output is HIGH only when both the inputs are HIGH.
